About the Owner
The name of the owner is Jetro A. Gurango. A first year student of Our Lady of Fatima University (Quezon City). He made a bakery to help his financial need for his studies. Jetro is a hardworking man that loves sweet and delicious food and of course the main example are bread and pastries and so because of his love in eating bread, that peaks the idea of building his own bakery. The bakery is stationed near his residence at Bagong Silangan Quezon City. At first he's hesitating to build such business for he is still a child and of course lack experience regarding the said business.
